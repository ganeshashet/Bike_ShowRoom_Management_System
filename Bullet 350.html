The Story

What started in 1932 continues to deliver pure motorcycling experiences across generations of riders. Bullet, the quintessential Royal Enfield, is today the longest running motorcycle in history to be in continuous production. A unique, hand painted fuel tank, the distinct thumb and a standing testimony to Royal Enfield's philosophy of simple design and resilient build make Bullet a Living legacy on two wheels.
